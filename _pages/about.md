Hi, I'm **Humoyunbek** — a Data Scientist at [HumbleBeeAI](#) in Incheon, South Korea, where I build applied machine-learning systems around **behavioral biometrics, fraud detection, and time-series & blockchain analytics**.

Over the past couple of years at HumbleBeeAI I've grown from Data Analyst to Head of Data Science to Data Scientist — founding the data-science team, leading a group of seven, and shipping work like behavioral bot-detection pipelines (keystroke and mouse dynamics), on-chain fraud detection across Cronos and Ethereum, investor-churn prediction for fintech, and time-series forecasting for offshore engineering. I work mostly in Python, SQL, scikit-learn, LightGBM, and BigQuery.

I'm also an honours student in Integrated System Engineering at **Inha University** and a recipient of the Global Scholarship 2.

Outside of work, I like writing — you'll find [tech notes and life thoughts on the blog](/blog/), and a [bookshelf](/books/) where I keep track of what I'm reading and what I made of it. Feel free to reach out.
